

Learning Goals:

1) Windows Forms UI Design:
	a) Working with Controls such as buttons, labels, and textboxes,
	b) Organizing the form layout (FlowLayoutPanel, Grid, etc)

2) Event Handling:
	a) Handling button clicks to input numbers and operators.
3) Basic Arithmetic Operations:
	a) Adding, Subtracting, multiplying and dividing numbers.
4) Clear / Reset Functionality:
	a) Ability to reset the calculator when needed.
